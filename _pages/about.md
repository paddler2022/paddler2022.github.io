Hi! I am **Yuheng Lu (卢昱恒)**, a first-year Master of Engineering student at the
Graduate School of Information, Production and Systems, [Waseda University](https://www.waseda.jp/top/en/),
Japan. I previously earned an **M.Sc. in Electrical &amp; Electronics Engineering** (minor in
Computer Science) from [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/en/), Switzerland, and a **B.Eng. in
Electrical Engineering &amp; Automation** from the [Harbin Institute of Technology](http://en.hit.edu.cn/), China.

My research sits at the intersection of **information retrieval** and **LLM-based agents**.
I build benchmarks and diagnostic analyses that expose where current retrievers and
deep-research agents break down — particularly in **multilingual and cross-lingual** settings —
and I am increasingly interested in **agentic systems** for tasks such as remote-sensing
image restoration.

I am always happy to talk about research. Feel free to reach out by
[email](mailto:luyuheng@akane.waseda.jp).
